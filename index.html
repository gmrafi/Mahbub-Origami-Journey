About the Book

“The Origami Journey: From Play to Purpose” is a visual step-by-step guide designed for complete beginners. The book includes creativity-based origami, engineering-style origami, and business origami, making it perfect for learners, hobbyists, and educators.

Kids Origami (7–10)

10 fun and easy patterns like paper boats, butterflies, cranes, and frogs — helping kids improve focus, patience, and creativity.

Engineering Origami (NASA-inspired)

15 advanced models like space structures, solar panels, and bridges that show how engineering and design work together.

Business Origami

Turn origami into a business model. Each fold teaches a simple business idea such as teamwork, innovation, and growth — building leadership and problem-solving skills.

Together, these sections make “The Origami Journey” not just a craft book, but a learning experience that connects education, art, and entrepreneurship. It also links to our Facebook page, “Origami Universe,” where readers can explore more designs and share their creations.

A simple piece of paper can teach creativity, patience, and innovation — essential for personal and professional growth.
